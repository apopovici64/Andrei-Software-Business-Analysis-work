Account Names must be in CAPITAL, and have max 20 characters.

CC_SSS_DDDD_PPPPP_XX

_ : (underscore) separetor char

Mandatory: 7 first characters ("CC_SSS_")

CC: 
2 characters from ISO Country Code 3166-1

SSS: 
Site: 3 characters AKA OHQ, FHQ, MoD, MHQ, MFA, etc...

XX:
Number: up to 2

-------------

Optional but strongly recommanded (depending on your organisation)

DDDD:
Dept: English up to 4 Char

PPPPP:
Pos: English Code up to 5
